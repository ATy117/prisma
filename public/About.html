Prisma is a web application made for users to save different color combinations as their reference for their future use. They may also use Prisma to look for inspiration on picking colors based from other users the palettes of the users they follow.
Node modules used:
bcryptjs | body-parser | cookie-parser | express | express-session | hbs | mongoose | nodemon


Other libraries used:
jquery | jscolor | moment | randomColor
Users may generate the colors of their palette by choosing a color from the color picker or typing the hex value of the color that they like. They also have to option to follow other users and like their palettes for future references.
